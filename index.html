About Our Center

Zowi Traditional Medical Center blends centuries-old healing traditions with contemporary healthcare practices to provide holistic care for body, mind, and spirit.

At Zowi, we believe in treating the root cause of illness rather than just symptoms. Our practitioners draw from diverse traditional healing systems including Bone-setting, Traditional Ethiopian Medicine, and Indigenous healing practices.

Holistic Approach

Natural Remedies

Personalized Care
